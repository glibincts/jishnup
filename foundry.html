Moulding with ARPA 300

Our state-of-the-art moulding process ensures high-quality castings through a systematic approach using the ARPA 300 machine.

1

Sand Mixing

Our advanced 110 tons/day capacity sand plant unit prepares premium green sand mixture with precise composition control.

2

Mold Preparation

The ARPA 300 machine ensures accurate and consistent mould formation with optimal compaction and dimensional stability.

3

Melting

Our high-efficiency induction furnaces of 500 Kg and 750 Kg capacities ensure precise temperature control and material composition.

4

Pouring & Cooling

Expert pouring techniques combined with controlled cooling ensure optimal casting properties and minimal defects.
